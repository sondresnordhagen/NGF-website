About Us

We are a new network for young people working in insurance intermediation in Norway, across companies, roles, and backgrounds.

Who are we?

NextGen Insurance Collective is a meeting place for young professionals in the insurance industry.

Based in Oslo, we aim to collaborate closely with insurance companies, reinsurers, brokers, and other leading industry organisations to create a platform where young talents can meet, share experiences, and lift each other up, regardless of their employer.

The network is open to everyone under 35 working in insurance intermediation in Norway.

Our Purpose

The goal is simple: to create an environment where young insurance intermediaries can grow professionally, build relationships, and help shape the future of the industry.

We organise informal afterwork gatherings, professional panels, and networking events throughout the year.
