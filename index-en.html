NextGen Insurance Collective

A new initiative for young professionals in insurance intermediation

We have identified a clear need for a shared meeting place for young professionals working in insurance. That is why we are launching a new network where young talents (35 and under) can meet, learn, and lift each other up. The goal is to create an environment for professional development and relationship-building across companies and roles.
